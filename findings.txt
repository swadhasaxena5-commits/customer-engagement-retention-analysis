PROJECT FINDINGS

1. Overall Churn Rate

* 20.37% of customers exited the bank.

2. Customer Engagement Impact

* Active customers churn rate: 14.27%
* Inactive customers churn rate: 26.85%
* Active customers are significantly more likely to stay.

3. Product Utilization Impact

* 1 Product: 27.71% churn
* 2 Products: 7.58% churn
* Customers with two products show the strongest retention.

4. Balance vs Loyalty

* Average balance of retained customers: 72,745
* Average balance of churned customers: 91,109
* High balance alone does not guarantee loyalty.

5. Credit Card Ownership Impact

* Without credit card: 20.81% churn
* With credit card: 20.18% churn
* Credit card ownership has minimal impact on retention.

6. Geography Analysis

* France: 16.15% churn
* Spain: 16.67% churn
* Germany: 32.44% churn
* Germany has the highest customer churn rate.
